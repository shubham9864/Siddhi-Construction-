About Us

Shidhi Construction is a leading construction company dedicated to building durable homes and commercial spaces. We combine innovation, quality, and trust to bring your dream projects to life.

Our mission is to deliver top-quality construction with modern architecture, and our vision is to be the most trusted construction company in the region.

Mr. Yogendra Rana, the Chairman and Founder, is a highly experienced professional with 15+ years of field expertise in the construction industry. He has successfully led multiple residential and commercial projects in Dehradun and across Uttarakhand, ensuring high-quality results and customer satisfaction. Under his leadership, Siddhi Construction & Interior has earned a reputation for deep work, transparency, and timely delivery.
